How it works

A structured intelligence layer between raw data and your decisions

01

22 adapters ingest live data

Every 1–60 minutes, specialised adapters pull from yFinance, FRED, EIA, GDELT, UCDP, USGS, SEC EDGAR, FCA, RSS feeds, and more. Each datum is normalised into a typed RawAtom.

02

Signal enrichment runs every 5 minutes

A second-order pass reads the raw atoms and emits derived signals — price regime, conviction tier, signal quality, macro confirmation, geopolitical risk exposure, and energy shock risk.

03

The knowledge graph resolves conflicts

Contradictory signals are detected and scored for epistemic stress. Confidence decays over time. The graph always reflects the current, weighted state of evidence — not stale cached values.

04

The copilot answers with grounded context

Your question is matched against the KB. Relevant atoms are retrieved, ranked, and injected into the LLM prompt alongside your portfolio. The answer cites the data it was built from.
