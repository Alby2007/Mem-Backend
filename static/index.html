HOW IT WORKS

A structured intelligence
layer between raw data
and your decisions.

01
22 adapters ingest live data
Every 1–60 minutes, specialised adapters pull from yFinance, FRED, EIA, GDELT, UCDP, USGS, SEC EDGAR, FCA, and more. Each datum is normalised into a typed, sourced atom.
02
Signal enrichment runs every 5 minutes
A second-order pass reads raw atoms and emits derived signals — price regime, conviction tier, signal quality, macro confirmation, geopolitical risk exposure, and energy shock risk.
03
The knowledge graph resolves conflicts
Contradictory signals are detected and scored for epistemic stress. Confidence decays over time. The graph always reflects the current, weighted state of evidence — not stale cached values.
04
You get grounded answers, not guesses
Your question is matched against the KB. Relevant atoms are retrieved, ranked, and injected into the LLM prompt alongside your portfolio. Every answer cites the data it was built from.
